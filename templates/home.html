About Us

At Pigment, our mission is to empower students to take control of their personal finances.

We understand that managing money can feel overwhelming. That’s why we’ve created a platform designed to simplify budgeting, track expenses, and set meaningful financial goals. By providing accessible tools and resources, we aim to help students build healthy financial habits that last a lifetime. Whether you're saving for tuition, planning a trip, or just trying to stay on top of your spending, we're here to guide you every step of the way.
